Magical Girl Raising Project: JOKERS

Magical Girl Raising Project is a light novel series by Asari Endou. Featuring illustrations by artist Marui-no, each story arc focuses on a group of sixteen Magical Girls who are stuck in a dangerous situation that requires them to either work together or turn against each other to survive. Magical Girl Raising Project: JOKERS is the fourth arc, comprising book 7.

Prism Cherry, who is painfully ordinary by Magical Girl standards, meets the Pure Elements, a mysterious team of princess Magical Girls who claim to be protecting S-City from monsters called Disruptors. The Pure Elements have attracted the attention of different groups within the Magical Kingdom, who all have their own vested interests in learning more about this suspicious new team.
